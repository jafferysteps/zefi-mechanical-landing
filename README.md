Premium, mobile-responsive landing page designed for **Zefi Mechanical Inc.** based in East Harlem, New York, NY.

*   **Branded Palette**: Deep navy blue (`#0f172a`) and steel blue (`#0284c7`) color palette.
*   **Interactive Estimator**: Select-driven price calculator for NYC sewer snaking and backflow DEP testing.
*   **NYC Master Plumber details**: Customized highlighting for DOB and DEP compliance codes.
*   **Sticky Header**: High-visibility branding logo and tap-to-call link.
*   **Omitted Sections**: Complying with rules, generic social links and vendor directories are completely omitted because no verified GMB records exist.
